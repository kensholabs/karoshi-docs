# Proposals

Every individual possessing an adequate amount of $ROSHI is eligible to initiate a proposal. There are no charges associated with creating a proposal, but it's crucial to maintain the required balance.

## Here are the prerequisites:
To put forth a proposal, you must hold 500 $ROSHI tokens.
A minimum quorum of 50% is mandatory.
At least 60% of votes are needed for acceptance.

## A proposal should comprise:
A title.
A description.
A deadline.
An optional action, such as executing a smart contract or making a decision.
