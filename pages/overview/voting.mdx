# Voting

![](/power.webp)

This subject can be intricate, and for a comprehensive understanding, we recommend reading [this excellent](https://jumpcrypto.com/square-root-voting/) article from Jump Crypto Research.
Voting options are limited to either in favor or against.
You have the flexibility to withdraw or alter your vote at any point.
When depositing, withdrawing, or voting in DAO, your complete balance of $ROSHI tokens will be used. Therefore, ensure you maintain only the necessary amount in the wallet you use for voting.
