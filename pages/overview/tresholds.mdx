# Tresholds

![](/trsh.png)

Membership in the DAO is automatically granted by possessing KAROSHI NFTs or $ROSHI tokens and accessing the DAO dashboard. KAROSHI holders will receive $ROSHI tokens via an airdrop. The thresholds are as follows:

- To activate the DAO: Hold $ROSHI tokens and access the DAO dashboard.
- To vote on proposals: Have at least 1 $ROSHI token in your wallet.
- To create a proposal: Hold 500 $ROSHI tokens.
- The minimum quorum: 50% of token holders.
- The minimum acceptance requirement: 60% of votes.
